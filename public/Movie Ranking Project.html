The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time

Forest Gump

Forrest Gump is a heartwarming and inspirational film that follows the extraordinary life of a mentally challenged man as he unwittingly influences and witnesses several pivotal moments in American history.

The Great Gatsby

The Great Gatsby is a visually stunning adaptation of F. Scott Fitzgerald's classic novel, exploring the decadence and disillusionment of the American Dream in the Roaring Twenties through the enigmatic millionaire Jay Gatsby's obsession with a lost love.

21 Jump Street

21 Jump Street is a hilarious and action-packed buddy comedy that follows two inept police officers who go undercover as high school students to infiltrate a drug ring, leading to uproarious situations and unexpected friendships.
